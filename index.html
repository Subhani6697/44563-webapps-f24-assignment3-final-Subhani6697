Vizag is my favorite city

Vizag is a beautiful coastal city in India, renowned for its stunning beaches, scenic hills, and vibrant culture. The city is known for its pristine beaches like R.K. Beach and Yarada Beach, the serene Ramakrishna Mission Beach, and the picturesque Araku Valley nearby. With its pleasant climate, bustling port, and rich cultural heritage, Vizag is a delightful destination that combines relaxation with exploration.

Places to Visit in Vizag

  1. RK Beach
  2. Kailasagiri Hill
  3. Simhachalam Temple

Nearby Places of Vizag

  • Lambasingi
  • Araku Valley
    • Stunning views of green hills and valleys.
    • Lovely waterfalls and peaceful surroundings.
  • Annavaram Temple
